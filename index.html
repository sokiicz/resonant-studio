Why Resonant Labs exists

Built without writing
a single line of code

I'm not a developer. I'm a digital marketer with too many ideas and a powerful new creative partner — Claude Code. Every app on this site was built entirely through conversation. I describe what I want, and the AI builds it. Not a single character was typed by me into any source file.

Since I didn't write the code, it felt wrong to monetize it. So I'm not. Everything here is completely free, open source, and available for anyone to use, fork, remix, or get inspired by.

My goal is simple: show what's possible. If a curious digital marketer with no coding skills can build this, imagine what you could create.
