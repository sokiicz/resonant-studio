Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Resonant Labs operates resonantstudio.online — a portfolio of web apps built entirely through AI collaboration and shared freely with everyone.

Contact: contactme@resonantstudio.online

What data we collect

We do not collect personal data directly. We have no user accounts, no login system, and no analytics tracking. The only data stored in your browser is:

  • Cookie consent preferences — stored in localStorage under ck_consent and ck_vid (a random visitor ID used to version your consent record). This data never leaves your device.

Cookies we use

Necessary (always active)

These are required for the site to function correctly and cannot be disabled.

NamePurposeExpires
ck_consentStores your cookie preferencesPersistent
ck_vidRandom ID to version your consentPersistent

Functional (optional)

These enable comment sections powered by Cusdis, an open-source comment system. When you enable this category, an iframe is loaded from cusdis.com which may set its own cookies. See the Cusdis Privacy Policy for details.

Third-party services

The site uses the following external services:

  • Cusdis (comments) — only loaded with your consent. Privacy policy →
  • Web3Forms (idea submission form) — only triggered when you actively submit the idea form. No tracking. Privacy policy →
  • GitHub (source code links) — external links only, no data is sent unless you click through. Privacy policy →

Your rights

You can withdraw or change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings button that appears in the bottom-right corner of any page.

Since we collect no personal data beyond your own device's localStorage, there is nothing to request deletion of on our end. Clearing your browser's localStorage will remove all stored preferences.

Changes to this policy

If we add new third-party services that set cookies, we will update this page and increment the consent version — which means you'll be asked to re-consent on your next visit.
